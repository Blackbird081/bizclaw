## 🎯 Vietnamese Legal Framework

### Key Laws for E-commerce/OPC
```
1. Luật Thương mại 2005 (Commercial Law)
2. Luật Cạnh tranh 2018 (Competition Law)
3. Luật Bảo vệ quyền lợi người tiêu dùng 2010
4. Nghị định 52/2013/NĐ-CP về TMĐT
5. Nghị định 85/2021/NĐ-CP (sửa đổi TMĐT)
6. Luật An ninh mạng 2018 (Cybersecurity Law)
7. Luật An toàn thông tin mạng 2015
```

### Regulatory Bodies
```
- Ministry of Industry and Trade (MOIT)
- Ministry of Information and Communications (MIC)
- Vietnam E-commerce Association (VNEDA)
- Consumer Protection Agency
- Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC)
```

## 📋 Contract Review Framework (6 Bước)

### Step 1: Parties Identification
```
Check:
- Full legal names
- Business registration numbers
- Addresses
- Authorized representatives
- Legal capacity

Red Flags:
- Incomplete entity info
- Unclear representation
- Foreign entity without license
```

### Step 2: Scope of Work
```
Check:
- Clear deliverables
- Timelines/deadlines
- Acceptance criteria
- Exclusions
- Dependencies

Red Flags:
- Vague scope
- Open-ended requirements
- "And other tasks as needed"
```

### Step 3: Payment Terms
```
Check:
- Amount and currency
- Payment schedule
- Payment conditions
- Late payment penalties
- Currency and exchange rate

VN Standard:
- 30% deposit
- 70% upon completion
- 5-10% retention
```

### Step 4: Liability & Risk
```
Check:
- Limitation of liability
- Indemnification clauses
- Force majeure
- Insurance requirements
- IP ownership

Red Flags:
- Unlimited liability
- Broad indemnification
- IP transfer without compensation
```

### Step 5: Termination Clauses
```
Check:
- Termination for convenience
- Termination for cause
- Notice periods
- Effect on payment
- Return of materials

Red Flags:
- One-sided termination
- Long notice without cause
- No payment upon termination
```

### Step 6: Dispute Resolution
```
Check:
- Governing law
- Dispute resolution method
- Jurisdiction
- Arbitration clause

VN Standard:
- Vietnamese law
- Local courts or Vietnam International Arbitration
```

## ⚠️ Common Legal Risks

### E-commerce Risks
```
1. False advertising → Consumer protection violation
2. Price misrepresentation → Penalties up to 100M VND
3. Data breach → Criminal liability + fines
4. Unfair contract terms → Unenforceable
5. Missing business license → Fines + shutdown
```

### Employment Risks
```
1. No labor contract → Criminal liability
2. Unpaid overtime → Labor law violation
3. No social insurance → Penalties
4. Wrong termination → Compensation required
```

### Contract Risks
```
1. Oral agreements → Hard to enforce
2. Missing signatures → Invalid contract
3. Vague terms → Disputes
4. No termination clause → Stuck in bad contract
```

## 🔍 Red Flags Checklist

### Contract Red Flags
```
⚠️ One-sided terms
⚠️ Unlimited liability
⚠️ IP ownership transfer
⚠️ Automatic renewal
⚠️ Excessive penalties
⚠️ Restrictive non-compete
⚠️ Data misuse rights
```

### Compliance Red Flags
```
⚠️ No data protection policy
⚠️ No terms of service
⚠️ Collecting data without consent
⚠️ Storing sensitive data insecurely
⚠️ No breach notification process
⚠️ Missing business registration
```
